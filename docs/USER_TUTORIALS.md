## Learning Flow Analyzer

The Learning Flow Analyzer helps you ensure your course has a logical progression and maintains learner engagement.

### How to Use:
1. Open your course in the **Editor**.
2. Click on the **Analytics** or **Pedagogy** tab in the sidebar.
3. Review the **Content Timeline**:
   - **Blue segments** represent text content.
   - **Green segments** represent interactive blocks.
   - **Orange segments** represent assessments.
4. Check the **Flow Dashboard** for issues like:
   - Missing introductions.
   - High cognitive load (too many text blocks in a row).
   - Missing final assessments.
5. Use the **Gap Detector** to see if any defined learning objectives are not covered by your content.

## Review Engine & Collaboration

Collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs) and stakeholders directly within the builder.

### How to Use:
1. Click the **Collaboration** icon in the top toolbar.
2. **Share your course**:
   - Click **Generate Link** to create a unique collaboration URL.
   - Send this link to your reviewers.
3. **Adding Comments**:
   - Select a block you want to discuss.
   - Type your comment in the collaboration panel.
   - Use `@username` to mention specific collaborators.
4. **Resolving Feedback**:
   - Once a change is made, click **Resolve** on the comment to archive it.

## Quality Scorecard

The Quality Scorecard provides an automated "health check" for your course content.

### How to Use:
1. Navigate to the **Project Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Quality Metrics**.
3. Review your scores in three key areas:
   - **Accessibility**: Checks for alt-text on images and videos.
   - **Readability**: Analyzes text complexity using Flesch-Kincaid standards.
   - **Engagement**: Measures the balance of interactive vs. passive content.
4. Click on **"View Details"** to see exactly which blocks need improvement.

## Learner Simulator

Predict how different types of learners will experience your course before you publish.

### How to Use:
1. Open the **Learner Simulator** from the advanced tools menu.
2. Select a **Learner Persona**:
   - **Fast Learner**: High accuracy, quick completion, prone to boredom.
   - **Typical Learner**: Average speed and engagement.
   - **Struggling Learner**: Needs more time, lower initial accuracy.
3. Click **Run Simulation**.
4. Analyze the results:
   - **Predicted Completion Time**: See if the course is too long or too short.
   - **Engagement Decay**: Identify where learners might lose interest.
   - **Predicted Score**: See if the assessments are too difficult for certain personas.
